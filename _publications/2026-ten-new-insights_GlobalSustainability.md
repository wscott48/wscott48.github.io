The 10 New Insights in Climate Science series is an annual synthesis of the most policy-relevant advances in climate research, produced jointly by Future Earth, the Earth League, and the World Climate Research Programme. The 2025 edition was released alongside COP30 and featured by the World Meteorological Organization and Carbon Brief.
